# VSCO 2 CE Mini Sample Bank

These files are derived from the official **VS Chamber Orchestra 2 Community Edition 2.1.1 - 50 Sample Pack** by Versilian Studios LLC.

- Source page: https://versilian-studios.com/vsco-community/
- Source archive: https://s3.amazonaws.com/VersilianStudios/50OrchestralSamples.rar
- License: Creative Commons Zero 1.0 Universal (CC0-1.0)
- Prepared format: 22,050 Hz, 16-bit, stereo PCM WAV

The preparation step only trims leading and trailing silence and converts the original 44,100 Hz PCM recordings to the project render rate. The original filenames are preserved.
